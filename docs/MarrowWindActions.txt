# Marrowwind Action Reference

This file summarizes the currently implemented Open Sea Tracker actions and rules. The app uses the term `repairMaterials` internally; some player-facing/action labels still say Repair Supplies.

## 0 Labor Actions

### Idle / Watch

- Takes no turn by itself and has no mechanical tracker effect unless the DM adjudicates one.
- Player-facing `Done In` / turns remaining displays a dash.

### Inventory Food

- Below-deck action.
- Reveals Food to the player view.
- If flooding penalties apply below deck, duration can increase.

### Inventory Water

- Below-deck action.
- Reveals Fresh Water to the player view.
- If flooding penalties apply below deck, duration can increase.

### Inventory Repair Supplies

- Below-deck action.
- Reveals Repair Supplies / Repair Materials to the player view.
- If flooding penalties apply below deck, duration can increase.

### Inventory Cargo

- Below-deck action.
- Lets the DM update or reveal cargo information through normal adjudication.
- If flooding penalties apply below deck, duration can increase.

### Navigate / Study Map

- Adjusts the Course Meter.
- Critical Failure: Course Meter +0.
- Failure: Course Meter +2.
- Success: Course Meter +4.
- Critical Success: Course Meter +5.
- Course Meter is capped at 12.
- Reveals Course State to players.
- Reveals Travel Remaining to players, rounded to the nearest 0.5 day.
- If this action resolves, skip the normal end-of-turn Course Meter decay for that turn.
- Navigator's Tools or Cartographer's Tools proficiency gives advantage.
- Sailor/Pirate background does not grant advantage by default.
- Perception is not a core navigation substitute.

### Investigate Bilge Rod

- Implemented action name: Examine Bilge Sounding Rod.
- Player-facing label: Investigate Bilge Rod.
- Below-deck action, but it does not receive the flooding extra-turn penalty.
- Reveals current Water Level to players.
- Success reveals Total Ingress immediately.
- Total Ingress is also revealed when Water Level is known two turns in a row.

### Fight Pack of Gulls

- Available only when the Pack of Gulls event is active.
- Event-specific action; resolve using the event prompt and DM adjudication.

### Collect Rainwater

- Available only when rainwater is available from an event.
- Event-specific action; resolve using the event prompt and DM adjudication.

## 1 Labor, 1 Player, 1 Turn Actions

### Recover Floating Wreckage

- Available only when the Floating Wreckage event is active.
- Costs 1 Labor.
- DC 12 Dexterity or Athletics.
- Success gains salvaged timber for DM adjudication.

### Salvage Lumber

- Costs 1 Labor.
- Takes 1 turn unless below-deck flooding penalties apply.
- Roleplay determines whether the salvage is above deck or below deck.
- The action includes an above/below-deck checkbox.
- If below deck and flooding, normal below-deck flooding penalties apply.
- Completion adds +2 Repair Materials.

### Man Helm

- Costs 1 Labor unless Calm Seas modifies the labor cost.
- If mast and rudder are both functional, roll Survival DC 12.
- Critical Success: Apply one Course State better for this turn and improve the Course Meter to the next better Course State.
- Success: Apply the current Course State travel effect.
- Failure: Apply one Course State worse for this turn only.
- Critical Failure: Apply one Course State worse for this turn only and reduce Course Meter by 1.
- If the ship is already Lost, one Course State worse still uses Lost.
- Sailor/Pirate background or Water Vehicles proficiency gives advantage.
- Navigator's Tools and Cartographer's Tools do not grant advantage on Helm.
- Broken Mast: no Helm check; action still costs Labor, but Helm applies no travel tick change.
- Broken Rudder with functional Mast: no Helm check; tracker randomly applies True Course, Drifting, Off Course, or Lost travel ticks, ignoring Course Meter.
- Broken Mast and Broken Rudder: use Broken Mast behavior.

### Operate Bilge Pump (Solo)

- Requires Bilge Pump Working.
- Costs 1 Labor.
- Strength DC 15.
- Failure: Water Level -2.
- Success: Water Level -3.

### Harpoon Fishing (Solo)

- Costs 1 Labor.
- Survival check.
- DC 18: +0.5 Food.
- DC 20: +1.0 Food.
- Fisherman background gives advantage.

### Reset Fishing Net

- Requires Net Tangled.
- Costs 1 Labor.
- Completion makes Net Ready.

### Repair Bilge Pump

- Requires Pump Jammed.
- Costs 1 Labor.
- Requires 1 Repair Material.
- Cannot be confirmed without enough Repair Materials.
- Completion makes the pump Working.

### Repair Rigging

- Requires Rigging Broken.
- Costs 1 Labor.
- Requires 1 Repair Material.
- Cannot be confirmed without enough Repair Materials.
- Creates a DC 13 Dexterity save prompt or 2d6 bludgeoning damage.

## 0 or 1 Labor, 1 Player, 2 Turn Actions

### Bucket Brigade

- Costs 1 Labor when started.
- Below-deck action.
- Takes 2 turns before flooding penalties.
- Completion reduces Water Level by 1 during water resolution.

### Recover

- Costs 0 Labor.
- Takes 2 turns.
- On completion, choose either Recover 2 Labor or Recover 1 Exhaustion.

## 1 Labor per Player, 2 Player Actions

### Operate Bilge Pump (Cooperative)

- Requires Bilge Pump Working.
- Requires 2 players.
- Costs 1 Labor per player.
- Both players roll Strength DC 15; only one success is required.
- No successes: Water Level -3.
- At least one success: Water Level -4.

### Cast Fishing Net

- Requires Net Ready.
- Requires 2 players.
- Costs 1 Labor per player.
- Each player rolls Survival DC 15.
- Each success adds +0.25 Food.
- After use, Net becomes Tangled.
- Fisherman background gives advantage to that participant.

### Harpoon Fishing (Cooperative)

- Requires 2 players.
- Costs 1 Labor per player.
- Both players roll Survival; the tracker uses the cooperative fishing outcomes.
- DC 15: +0.5 Food.
- DC 18: +1.0 Food.
- DC 20: +2.0 Food.
- Fisherman background gives advantage to that participant.

### Repair Active Leak

- Requires at least 1 Active Leak.
- Requires 2 players per leak-repair group.
- Costs 1 Labor per player before flooding penalties.
- Requires 1 Repair Material per leak repaired.
- Cannot be confirmed without enough Repair Materials.
- Below-deck flooding penalties apply.
- Completion removes 1 Active Leak per repair group.

## 2 Labor per Player, 2 Players, 2 Turn Actions

### Repair Mast

- Requires Mast Broken.
- Requires 2 players.
- Costs 2 Labor per player when started.
- Takes 2 turns before flooding penalties.
- Requires 1 Repair Material.
- Cannot be confirmed without enough Repair Materials.
- Completion sets Mast to Repaired.

### Repair Rudder

- Requires Rudder Broken.
- Requires 2 players.
- Costs 2 Labor per player when started.
- Takes 2 turns before flooding penalties.
- Requires 1 Repair Material.
- Cannot be confirmed without enough Repair Materials.
- Completion sets Rudder to Repaired.

## Labor and Exhaustion Rules

- Each character can safely accumulate up to 3 Labor.
- Taking a labor action while already at or above the safe threshold creates a Constitution save prompt after the action starts.
- Labor save DC is `10 + ((Labor After Action - 3) * 2)`, capped at 20.
- Failure adds 1 Exhaustion.
- Success avoids Exhaustion but still keeps the Labor gained.

| Labor After Action | CON DC |
| --- | --- |
| 4 | 12 |
| 5 | 14 |
| 6 | 16 |
| 7 | 18 |
| 8+ | 20 |

## Water System

- Minimum Water Ingress starts at 1.
- Total Ingress is Minimum Water Ingress + Active Leaks.
- At the water step: `Water Level = Water Level + Total Ingress - Pumping - Buckets`.
- Water Level cannot drop below 0.
- Below 5, players only see that water is in the bilge and the ship is safe unless the bilge rod reveals the exact value.
- At 5 or higher, players automatically know the visible water level.
- Cargo Hold flooding: below-deck actions take +1 turn.
- Waist Deep flooding: below-deck actions cost +1 Labor.
